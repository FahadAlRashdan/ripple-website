Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 2026

Who we are

Ripple Intelligence (“Ripple”, “we”) is an AI restaurant-intelligence platform for the food & beverage sector in Kuwait and the GCC. This policy covers both this website and, at a high level, how the Ripple platform treats data. Questions: hello@ripple.kw.

What this website collects

This site has no accounts, no advertising trackers and no analytics cookies. It stores one thing in your browser: your language preference (English or Arabic), kept locally on your device and sent to no one. If you contact us, the message travels through your own email app — we receive only what you choose to send.

What the Ripple platform collects

The platform reads publicly available information only: public social posts, comments and captions, public customer reviews, public creator content, and industry news and podcasts. It never accesses private accounts, private messages or anything behind a login. Client workspace data lives in an isolated tenant, visible only to that client’s team.

How market intelligence stays anonymous

Market-level insights are published as cuisine- and area-level aggregates. No competitor brand or branch is ever named in shared market intelligence, and no individual reviewer is profiled.

Retention

Raw collected social data is kept on a limited horizon — long enough to serve the reporting windows that use it — while derived aggregates are kept longer as the historical record, and operational logs are kept for a shorter debugging window. Exported reports carry a watermark with the client name and timestamp.

Sharing

We do not sell data. We use service providers (hosting, AI analysis, email delivery) strictly to operate the platform, under their own confidentiality obligations.

Your choices

For questions about the information we hold, or to ask about access or removal, write to hello@ripple.kw. This policy is governed by the laws of the State of Kuwait.
